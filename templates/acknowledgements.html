Acknowledgements

Soviet Journals Reconnected was developed during a yearlong fellowship at the Center for Digital Humanities at Princeton University. The digitized contribution-level citations from the Soviet Index of Periodical Publications was kindly provided by Indiana University Libraries Digital Projects & Services. The dataset was complimented with bibliographical data (especially print runs and editors), collected at the Princeton University Library.

This site was developed as a Flask App on the basis the Mapping Genres Website (Brown University Library). The full code of the website, as well as the SJR dataset can be found on Github.

I would particularly like to thank the following people for their support, expressed in many hands-on consultations and development meetings: Jean Bauer, Natalia Ermolaev, Ben Hicks, Rabea Gleissner, Thomas Keenan and Katherine Hill Reischl.
